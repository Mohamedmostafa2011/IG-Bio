🔬 Steps of Resistance

  1. Some of the bacteria are resistant to antibiotics.
  2. This means that when you treat the bacteria, only the non-resistant strains of bacteria are killed.
  3. The individual resistant bacteria will survive and reproduce, so the population will increase.
  4. This resistant strain could cause a serious infection that can't be treated by antibiotics.
