Ciliated Cell

Animal

Function: Protection

  • Found in Trachea & Bronchi.
  • Has tiny hair-like projections (Cilia).
  • Beats rhythmically to move mucus (containing dust/bacteria) away from lungs.

Root Hair Cell

Plant

Function: Absorption

  • Long extension increases Surface Area.
  • Absorbs Water (Osmosis) and Mineral Ions (Active Transport).
  • No chloroplasts (underground).

Palisade Cell

Plant

Function: Photosynthesis

  • Found in the upper part of the leaf.
  • Packed with Chloroplasts containing chlorophyll.
  • Column-shaped to fit many near the surface.

Red Blood Cell

Animal

Function: O2 Transport

  • Biconcave Shape: Increases Surface Area.
  • No Nucleus: More space for Haemoglobin.
  • Contains Haemoglobin to bind Oxygen.

Nerve Cell (Neurone)

Animal

Function: Conduction

  • Long Axon: Carry impulses long distances.
  • Myelin Sheath: Insulates electrical signal.
  • Branched ends to connect to other nerves.

Sperm Cell

Animal (Gamete)

Function: Reproduction

  • Flagellum (Tail): To swim to the egg.
  • Mitochondria: Provide energy for swimming.
  • Acrosome: Enzymes to digest egg coating.
